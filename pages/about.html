PlaceTrack was built because students deserved better than word-of-mouth placement advice and scattered Excel sheets. We centralise, visualise, and democratise campus recruitment data.

Why PlaceTrack exists

Every year, thousands of students enter placement season without a clear picture of what companies expect, what their peers earned last year, or whether they even qualify for a company before applying.

PlaceTrack was started in 2019 by a group of final-year engineering students who collected, cleaned, and published their college's 5-year placement history — and saw 200+ students use it in the first week.

Today, the platform serves students across Engineering, MBA, General Degree, and Diploma institutions — helping them make data-driven decisions about their careers.
