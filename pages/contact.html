Frequently asked questions
How often is the placement data updated? +
Data is updated at the end of each placement season — typically between December and March. We also make corrections throughout the year when reported by students or the placement cell.
Can I submit my placement data to be added? +
Yes! If you've been placed recently and your data isn't reflected, use the contact form above with subject "Add a company" and we'll verify and update it.
Is the eligibility checker 100% accurate? +
The checker is based on historical eligibility criteria from past seasons. Companies may change their criteria year to year — always verify with your placement cell for the current season.
Does this portal support all types of institutions? +
Yes — PlaceTrack supports Engineering colleges, MBA institutes, General Degree colleges (BCA, B.Com, BBA, B.Sc), and Diploma/Polytechnic institutions. Switch institution type in the Eligibility Checker.
